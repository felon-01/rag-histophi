The Ottoman Empire (1299â€“1922) was a major Islamic empire centered in Anatolia and southeast Europe. At its height under Suleiman the Magnificent, it controlled territories across three continents.

The empire integrated diverse cultures and religions through the millet system. Its military innovations, architecture, and administrative structures were highly influential.
